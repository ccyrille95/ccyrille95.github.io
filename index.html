  • About Me

    Data driven problem solver by day, Fashion savant by night.
    Always personable.

    Who am I?

    I am originally from Pompano Beach, Florida. My parents immigrated to America from Haiti before I was born. They always taught me to be an opportunist. Leaving their families in Haiti was a risk they were happy to take, knowing my sisters and I would have a better upbringing in the USA.
    In 2013, I left Pompano Beach for Tallahassee to attend Florida State University. I was a first-generation college student. I faced trials and tribulations with my chin up - remembering to find the opportunity for growth with each challenge.

    Interactive Resume

    What do I do?

    Before attending FSU, I had no idea what databases were. I knew my online information was stored somewhere, but never really thought about it. My sister, who’s a database engineer, introduced me to data science while I was having trouble picking a major. She sold it to me as a “hot field that no one knows about.” I fell in love with it.

    I’m a naturally curious person, and looking at data allows me to question and prove trends in the real world. I started applying data analytics to my every day hobbies, like fashion and sneakers, to create my fashion blog @CanopyRoadRTL. I created an app (more info on Featured tab) that lets me see what trends people are discussing on Reddit.
